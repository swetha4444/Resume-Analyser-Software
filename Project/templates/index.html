About


The project consist of two parts- Student and Recruiter. The student and upload his/her resume and get job insights whereas the recruiter can upload a bunch of resumes and get a summarized report on the resumes which makes it easy for him to choose the candidates without having to go through each resume line by line.

SERVICES

What we offer


Parses the resume (pdf, txt, word file) and retrieves details.

Summarises the description and work-experiences and retrieves key words.

Batch process a set of resumes and catogerise applicants based on skills and other factors.



Data Analysis on batch processed resume.

Knowledge graph of resume skills and work experience.

Resume based job classification.
